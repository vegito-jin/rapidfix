About Us

At ‘Physio Wellness’ we specialize in providing Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation to our patients. We are in the service of not only treating the aches and pains, but also to provide you with the very best care and therapy that’s personalized for your recovery plan.

Consultations with an expert physiotherapist at our physiotherapy and rehabilitation clinic is always one-on-one, beginning with an initial assessment, expert evaluation and appropriate treatment.

We believe in providing a Holistic Therapy plan with timely set goals to get you back on track living life to the fullest. Our therapy plans include Biomechanical and Posture Assessment, Manual therapies (like Mayo facial Release, soft tissue and joint Manipulations), Physiotherapy, European Acupuncture or Dry Needling, Marma therapy, Cranio Sacral Therapy and Rehabilitation.

Our modalities include Advanced laser, IFT, TENS and all therapeutic currents, ultrasonic, Long Wave Diathermy with exercise rehabilitation.
